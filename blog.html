Post #1: Data Analysis

01/20/2017

Mod-1 is complete...hooray! Throughout mod-1 I continually pushed myself to learn new concepts and become proficient at them through research, application, and repitition. I learned more in six weeks than I though possible and am very satsfied with the quality of education I'm receiving at Turing. One subject that particularly piqued my interest was data analysis.

Our final project, Headcount, tackled this concept head on. We were given CSV files from which we had to store data in respective repositories. From these repositories we analyzed variance, correlation, and other statistical occurances between sets of data. Having majored in Economics in college, data analysis (regression in particular) fascinates me. I hope to further progress my skills in this area in software development and am keeping data science in mind for future scholastic pursuits.
