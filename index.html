Born and raised in Northeast Ohio, from a young age I held a fascination with computers and technology. I taught myself to be proficient in a wide variety of consumer tech products and founded a tech service business while in high school. I am college educated and hold degrees in accounting and economics from Ohio University. After venturing into the accounting world, I realized that I was neither doing what I loved or tangibly helping people. I then pursued tech service full-time as the sole proprietor of R.S. Tech in Chautauqua, NY and most recently Denver, CO. I am now pursuing my passion for software development in the Back-End Engineering program at the Turing School of Software and Design in Denver, CO. In the future I hope to join my passion for making a difference in people's lives with pursuits in software development.
